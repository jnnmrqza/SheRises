SheRises

SheRises is a non-governmental organization with purpose of helping and empowering women. It stands as a beacon of hope and empowerment for women across the Philippines. It's dedicated to help in fostering one's growth and independence by offering job oppurtunities and nurturing their skills. It aims to raise and strengthen the voices of women facing various challenges in a world full of judgments about women's abilities.
